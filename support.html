Escher Blocks - Support

About Escher Blocks

Escher Blocks is an isometric drawing app for creating 3D cube structures and line art on your iOS and Android devices.

Getting Help

If you have questions, encounter issues, or need assistance with Escher Blocks, we're here to help.

Contact Support

Email us at: support@schr.app

We typically respond within 1-2 business days.

Common Questions

How do I save my drawings?
Your work is automatically saved. You can also manually save drawings from the Projects screen.

How do I export my artwork?
Tap the export button to save your drawings as PNG images or .schr files that can be shared and reopened.

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?
Try restarting the app first. If the issue persists, please contact us at support@schr.app with details about the problem and your device model.

Feature Requests

We welcome your feedback and suggestions! Send your ideas to support@schr.app.

Privacy

We respect your privacy. Escher Blocks does not collect or share your personal information. All drawings are stored locally on your device.
