Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my work?

Your sketches are automatically saved to your device as you work. You can also export them as images.

Is it free?

Escher Blocks is free to download and use. The Basic version includes core features at no cost. Premium features can be unlocked by upgrading to the Pro version through an in-app purchase.
